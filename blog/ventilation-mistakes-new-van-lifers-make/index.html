Mistake 1: No Crossflow Plan
#

A roof fan alone is not enough. Pair extraction with side-window intake.

Mistake 2: Buying by CFM Only
#

Noise, controllability, and reliability matter as much as airflow rating.

Mistake 3: Skipping Moisture Strategy
#

You need airflow in cold weather too, not just summer heat.

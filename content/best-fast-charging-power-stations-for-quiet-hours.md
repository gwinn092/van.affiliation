This page is for that constraint. Not the spec sheet — the timing.

We set a **700Wh minimum floor** and rank by **predictable refill windows**, not raw capacity. The picks below are sorted into three buckets by the constraint that's actually limiting you: short outlet windows, rule-heavy timing, or needing more margin so one rough night doesn't cascade. Find your constraint, pick your bucket, check the price. That's the whole decision.

## What "quiet-hours" really means here

Quiet hours is not just a noise rule. It's a **recovery constraint**:

- Your main win is **predictable refill timing** (so a controlled window still gets you back to stable).
- Your second win is **repeatability** (so you can run the same routine under the same rules every week).
- Your trap is buying the biggest Wh and still missing windows because the timing problem wasn't solved.

If you're under the 700Wh floor (or you're already drifting), use **[Under-$1000 Upgrade Plan](/under-1000-upgrade-plan/)** first.

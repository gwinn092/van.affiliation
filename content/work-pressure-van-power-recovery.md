## The failure pattern (what changes under work pressure)

- Longer stretches of "always on" devices.
- Less flexibility for driving, shore, or solar timing.
- Quiet hours and calls compress recovery windows.

- Define what must stay on to complete work and sleep safely.
- Define what becomes optional until stability returns.
- If sharing a rig, align on baseline before stress rises.

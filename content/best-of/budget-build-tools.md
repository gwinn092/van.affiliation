## How We Picked

We rank tools by frequency of use, install quality impact, and whether they reduce expensive rework.

## What to Look For

- Ergonomics for long work sessions
- Replacement part and battery availability
- Accuracy over flashy features

## Top Picks

### Core Set for Most Builders
Drill/driver, jigsaw, measuring kit, clamps, safety gear.

### Electrical Set for Safe Installs
Quality crimper, heat-shrink, ferrules, multimeter.

### Fastener Set for Modular Builds
Rivnut tool and torque wrench for serviceable mounting.

## FAQ

### Should I buy or rent specialty tools?
Rent or borrow when usage is rare.

### What tool is most underrated?
A good crimper. Bad crimps cause hidden electrical failures.

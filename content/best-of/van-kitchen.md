## How We Picked

We favor gear that is easy to clean, easy to store, and durable enough for vibration-heavy travel.

## What to Look For

- Multi-use tools over single-purpose gadgets
- Stable cooking surfaces
- Minimal cleanup time
- Low replacement cost

## Top Picks

### Best Budget Stove Setup
Simple two-burner systems still deliver the best value.

### Best Prep + Storage Combo
A compact knife board, stackable containers, and one pan does most of the work.

### Best Upgrade After 90 Days
Add refrigeration only after validating your daily cooking routine.

## FAQ

### Is an indoor sink required?
No, many people use portable water setups effectively.

### Is propane safe in a van?
It can be, with proper storage, leak checks, and ventilation.

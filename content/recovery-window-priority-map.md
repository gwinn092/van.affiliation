When power feels tight, most people ask the wrong question:
> “How many watt-hours do I have?”

The better question is:
> “What’s my best **recovery window** today?”

This page helps you choose the recovery move that matches your reality—**shore**, **drive recovery**, or **solar behavior**—without guessing.

If you want the full ladder this fits into, start here: **[Van Power Recovery Stack](/van-power-recovery-stack/)**.

---

## The recovery-first rule (CALV standard)

Priority is not “biggest battery.” Priority is **time-to-recover**:
- the move you can actually execute
- the move that restores margin reliably
- the move that reduces stress and conflict

- **[Bluetti Fast Charging](/bluetti-fast-charging/)**

## Step 1: Pick today’s day type (don’t overthink it)

Choose the closest match:

1) **Parked day** (you’re staying put)  
2) **Move day** (you’ll drive)  
3) **Workday** (baseline must stay stable)  
4) **Bad conditions day** (clouds, heat, humidity, or you’re already behind)  
5) **Constrained night** (quiet hours / stealth limits recovery options)

Mode check first:
- **[Daily Van Power Mode System](/daily-van-power-mode-system/)**

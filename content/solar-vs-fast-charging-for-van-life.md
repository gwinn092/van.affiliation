## When solar is the right fix

- You have consistent sun exposure and can hold a daily capture window.
- You can realistically plan around shade constraints in the places you stay.

## When fast charging is the right fix

- Harvest is inconsistent due to clouds, winter, shade, work schedule, or repeated bad weeks.
- You need time-to-recover on demand instead of waiting for ideal capture conditions.

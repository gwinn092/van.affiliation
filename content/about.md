I started van life to lower living costs, travel longer, and build a lifestyle around remote work freedom. After overspending on my first setup, I rebuilt everything around one rule: buy fewer, better-value essentials.

This site exists to help new van lifers avoid expensive mistakes. Every guide focuses on practical budgets, reliable gear, and realistic tradeoffs so you can get on the road with confidence.

Mission:
- Share budget-first van life gear recommendations.
- Publish beginner-friendly setup guides.
- Keep affiliate content transparent and useful.

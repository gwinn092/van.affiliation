## Step 1 — Choose the right kind of refill window (reality-first)

Examples include campground access, a friend’s driveway, a paid RV spot, or other permitted outlet access.

Stay rules-first:
- Follow property rules and posted access limits.
- Do not trespass or use outlets without permission.
- Do not overload outlets or extension setups.
- Follow manufacturer guidance for charging behavior.

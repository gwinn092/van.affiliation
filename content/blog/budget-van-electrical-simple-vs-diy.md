The right electrical strategy depends on your actual usage, not social media build standards.

## Simple System (Power Station)

Best if you want fast setup, low risk, and minimal wiring. Cost is predictable and troubleshooting is easier.
We’re keeping this high-level—no wiring instructions here. For installs, follow manufacturer guidance or work with a qualified installer.

## Full DIY System

Best if you need high sustained loads, large battery banks, or long-term full-time use with heavy appliances.

## Budget Reality

For many beginners, a mid-tier power station plus modest solar is the best first step.

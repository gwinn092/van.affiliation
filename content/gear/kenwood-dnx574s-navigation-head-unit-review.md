This review covers the Kenwood DNX574S from that real-use perspective.

## Why We Chose It

I chose this unit because it balances practical navigation functionality with strong smartphone integration. In van life, navigation is not just destination routing. It is route risk management:

- avoiding low clearances,
- planning fuel and stop strategy,
- managing long reroutes without chaos.

A good head unit also anchors your driving workflow for calls, media, and map transitions.

## Installation Notes from a 2017 ProMaster 2500 Build

A head unit upgrade is mostly an integration project. The hardware is only half the story. In my build experience, best results came from:

- quality harness adapters,
- proper dash kit fitment,
- steering wheel control retention planning,
- camera input and trigger wiring done cleanly.

If these details are rushed, even a great unit feels average.

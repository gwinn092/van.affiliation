## The fridge-driven drift pattern (what people mistake)

Fridge-related drift is usually a pattern problem, not one single event:
- "Nothing changed," but battery falls faster than expected.
- Heat and humidity can keep cooling demand elevated.
- Clouds and parking constraints can shrink refill opportunities.

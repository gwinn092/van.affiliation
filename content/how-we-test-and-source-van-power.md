Create A Van Life is climate-tested and recovery-first. The goal is simple: keep your system recoverable under real conditions, not just on ideal days.

We use **CAVL** as shorthand for **Create A Van Life**.

## Our #1 ranking rule: time-to-recover beats raw capacity

Storage matters, but refill reliability usually decides whether a week stays stable.
We prioritize repeatable recovery windows you can actually execute.

Use:
- **[Recovery Window Priority Map](/recovery-window-priority-map/)**
- **[Recovery Moves Index](/recovery-moves-index/)**

## How we label claims (no invented numbers)

- **Manufacturer specs and claims:** explicitly labeled and linked to official product pages.
- **Third-party tests:** explicitly labeled and linked to independent reviewers.
- **Lived experience / Field Notes:** clearly framed as experience, not universal guarantees.

Example format:
- **[Best Fast-Charging Power Stations](/best-fast-charging-power-stations/)**

## Our minimum recommendation rule (700Wh+)

For recommendation pages, we keep a 700Wh+ minimum as a baseline floor.

Context:
- **[What 700Wh Actually Runs](/what-700wh-actually-runs/)**

## Price drift policy

Pricing changes often, so we use an “under now” vs “watch list” structure.
We do not promise a model will always stay under a fixed price cap.

Example:
- **[Portable Power Stations Under $500](/best-of/power-stations-under-500/)**

## Safety policy (high-level only)

We keep power guidance high-level and recovery-focused.
We do not publish wiring tutorials; for installs, follow manufacturer guidance or work with a qualified installer.

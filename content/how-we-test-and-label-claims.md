Create A Van Life uses a **recovery-first** lens: we rank products by how reliably they help you recover from drift and return to a stable weekly routine, not by headline specs alone.

We keep a **700Wh+ minimum floor** because lower-capacity units are more likely to force charge-chasing in bad weather or tight refill windows. The goal is repeatable stability, not one good day.

We prioritize **recharge speed and time-to-recover** over raw Wh because real van routines are constrained by outlet access, rules, and schedule. A unit that recovers quickly in your actual window is usually a better buy than a larger unit with slower refill behavior.

## Claims labels we use

- **Spec:** manufacturer-published specification (input/output, capacity class, stated charge behavior).
- **Reported:** recurring pattern seen across credible user reporting, brand materials, and third-party reviews.
- **Measured:** direct observation from our own testing workflow or instrumented checks.
- **Estimate:** clearly modeled assumption used for planning, not a guarantee.

## Boundaries

This site is buying and troubleshooting focused.  
We do **not** publish step-by-step electrical wiring procedures.

## Update policy

We review this framework **monthly** and update sooner when one of these triggers appears:

- a new model release in a covered tier,
- meaningful firmware behavior change,
- major published spec revision or correction.

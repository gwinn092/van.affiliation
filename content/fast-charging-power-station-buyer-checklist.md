## Step 3 — Avoid the spec traps (what to verify)

Verify claims with manufacturer docs and third-party testing before you buy.

- “Fast charge” behavior: verify mode limits and whether special input conditions are required.
- Heat and noise tradeoffs: verify how aggressive modes affect comfort in your use case.
- Pass-through behavior and use while charging: verify model-specific guidance; do not assume behavior.
- Port mix fit: make sure AC and DC output paths match your daily workflow.

When battery is tight, the fight isn’t usually about power.

It’s about uncertainty:
- “What should we do next?”
- “Is this fair?”
- “Are we about to ruin tomorrow?”

This page gives you a fast **recovery window script** you can run together to choose one refill move calmly—without debating every detail.

## What this script does

This script helps you do three things quickly:
1) pick a mode (Normal / Conservation / Recovery)
2) choose one recovery move (shore / drive / solar behavior)
3) agree on one boundary so you don’t drift again

Mode system:
- **[Daily Van Power Mode System](/daily-van-power-mode-system/)**

If you’re already reactive, use the reset first:
- **[Van Power Conflict Reset Script](/van-power-conflict-reset-script/)**

## The 60-second Recovery Window Script (say it out loud)

### Step 1: Name the moment (10 seconds)
> “We’re not doing a full debate right now. We’re choosing **one recovery window** to get stable.”

### Step 2: Choose the mode (10 seconds)
> “For today, are we in **Normal**, **Conservation**, or **Recovery**?”

Use:
- **[Daily Van Power Mode System](/daily-van-power-mode-system/)**

If either person says “I feel behind,” assume **Recovery** until you regain margin.

### Step 3: Choose the day type (10 seconds)
> “What kind of day is this: parked, move day, workday, bad conditions, or constrained night?”

Use the chooser:
- **[Recovery Window Priority Map](/recovery-window-priority-map/)**

### Step 4: Pick ONE recovery move (15 seconds)
> “What’s our best refill move today: **shore**, **drive recovery**, or **solar behavior**?”

Then commit:
> “We pick one move and commit to it until the next check.”

Recovery frameworks:
- **[Shore Power Recovery Routine](/shore-power-recovery-routine/)**  
- **[Solar vs Alternator Charging](/solar-vs-alternator-charging/)**  
- **[Cloudy Week Power Recovery Plan](/cloudy-week-power-recovery-plan/)**  
- **[Bluetti Fast Charging](/bluetti-fast-charging/)**  

### Step 5: Choose ONE boundary (15 seconds)
> “What’s one boundary that prevents drift while we recover?”

Pick one:
- “Baseline stays.”
- “Optional comfort is conditional.”
- “We stop one leak today.”
- “We pause one category until we’re stable.”

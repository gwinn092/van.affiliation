This checklist helps you slow down just enough to buy the **right fix**, not the most emotionally satisfying fix.

## Before you buy (5-minute checklist)

### ✅ 1) Confirm your system is above the baseline floor
CALV minimum for recommendations: **700Wh+**.

Baseline context:
- **[What 700Wh Actually Runs](/what-700wh-actually-runs/)**

If you’re below the floor, your first “upgrade” is usually just getting out of the underpowered trap.

---

### ✅ 2) Name your real bottleneck (don’t guess)
Pick the one that matches reality:

- **Recovery speed:** “We can’t refill fast enough after a normal day.”  
  Use: **[Recovery Window Priority Map](/recovery-window-priority-map/)**

- **Night drift:** “We wake up behind (heat/humidity/quiet hours).”  
  Use: **[Overnight Van Power Checklist](/overnight-van-power-checklist/)**  
  and condition pages:
  - **[Heat-Wave Van Power Recovery](/heat-wave-van-power-recovery/)**
  - **[Humidity Night Power Stability](/humidity-night-power-stability/)**
  - **[Quiet Hours Recovery Plan](/quiet-hours-recovery-plan/)**

- **Workday instability:** “Workdays fail and we panic late-day.”  
  Use: **[Workday Power Boundaries](/workday-power-boundaries/)**  
  plus: **[Midday Van Power Check](/midday-power-check/)**

- **Conflict/fairness:** “We argue about comfort and priorities.”  
  Use: **[Van Power Comfort Budget](/van-power-comfort-budget/)**  
  and: **[Van Power Conflict Reset Script](/van-power-conflict-reset-script/)**

If you don’t name the bottleneck, upgrades turn into gambling.

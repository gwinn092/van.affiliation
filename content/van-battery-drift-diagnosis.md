If your power keeps sliding even when each day feels "normal," this page helps you diagnose the cause and fix it in the right order.

## The 3 drift causes (most common)

Most recurring drift comes from one or more of these:
1) **Leaks** (hidden drain)  
2) **Baseline creep** (more "must-haves" than your routine supports)  
3) **Weak recovery windows** (you cannot refill reliably)

## Step 2 — Find the leak (the "doing nothing" test, high-level)

No measurements needed. Look for behavioral flags:
- Power drops faster when you think "nothing changed"
- Convenience AC paths stay active by default
- Always-on habits stay active without intention

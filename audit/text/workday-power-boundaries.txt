Step 1: Define your Work Baseline (protected category) # Work baseline is what you need to do the job without scrambling. Write baseline as categories, not devices: essential computing + essential connectivity minimum lighting for focused work the simplest comfort needed to stay functional (climate matters) Heat note: baseline expands in heat because sleep and focus collapse without it.
